SMITHY

The smithy, a crucial hub of industry, boosts our civilization's iron generation by 10%, ensuring a steady supply of this vital resource. Skilled blacksmiths forge raw iron into essential tools and weapons, enhancing our infrastructure and military strength. Strategically located, it maximizes resource efficiency, driving the growth of our cities and armies. With each forged item, the smithy fortifies our foundation, propelling progress and stability throughout our society.

Cost of the building:

  • Food: 5000
  • Iron: 10000
  • Wood: 12000
