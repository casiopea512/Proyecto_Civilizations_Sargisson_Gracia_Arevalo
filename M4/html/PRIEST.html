PRIEST

The priest, devoid of both attack and defense capabilities, serves as a beacon of support and sanctification on the battlefield. While a priest remains in our army and there's sufficient mana, our current units (those created afterward won't be sanctified) can be blessed, enhancing their stats. However, should all priests perish, the sanctification on our units will cease, leaving them vulnerable once more. They are the guardians of morale and resilience, ensuring our forces stand steadfast in the face of adversity.
